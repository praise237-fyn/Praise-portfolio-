My Story

My name is Azinwi Praise Ntambu born on the 15th of june 2005.I am a Cameroonian and a level 300 student at the prestigious University of Bamenda. My journey in healthcare began with a deep-rooted passion for community impact and helping others. Through my nursing and midwifery background, I've dedicated myself to providing compassionate care while continuously growing professionally.

What sets me apart is my dynamic personality that seamlessly blends healthcare expertise with creative interests. Whether I'm caring for patients, playing bass guitar, or designing web interfaces, I bring the same level of dedication and innovative thinking to everything I do.

My philosophy centers on the belief that healthcare and technology can work together to create better outcomes for communities. This drives my volunteer work as a frontend web designer at XUMA, where I help bridge the gap between healthcare services and digital accessibility.
